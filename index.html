About Interim Health

As a dedicated subsidiary of Interim Consulting, Interim Health brings over two decades of corporate governance, recruitment excellence, and strategic management directly to the South African healthcare sector.

Our mission is to bridge the critical gap in healthcare staffing by providing highly qualified, thoroughly vetted medical professionals. Whether your facility requires emergency locum nurses, dedicated caregivers, or permanent specialized medical staff, we deliver operational continuity with uncompromised quality and compliance.
